ABOUT I-WEEK'26

I-WEEK’26 is a power-packed four-day mega celebration hosted by the Innopreneurs Club of PSG College of Arts & Science, where innovation meets creativity and ideas come alive. From February 23 to 26, 2026, the campus transforms into a buzzing hub of challenges, collaborations, and exciting experiences that spark entrepreneurial spirit, inspire fresh thinking, and bring passionate minds together. The valediction ceremony will take place on 26th february.
